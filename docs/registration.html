Terms and Conditions

  • Registration Includes:

    • Full Registration: Reception on May 18, banquet on May 20, lunch and coffee breaks on May 19 and 20, and access to all technical programs.
    • Guest/Companion Registration: Reception on May 18 and banquet on May 20.
    • Payment Deadline: Payments must be received and processed by May 4; unpaid registrations will be canceled.

  • Paper Submission:

    • At least one author of an accepted paper must complete a full registration for the paper to be included in the program and proceedings.
    • A maximum of two papers can be associated with a single registration.

  • No-shows:

    • Registrants who do not attend the conference and fail to cancel in advance will not receive a refund.

  • Data Protection:

    • Personal information collected during registration will only be used for conference-related purposes and will not be shared without consent.

By completing the registration process, you agree to these terms and conditions. For inquiries, please contact .

Cancellation Policy

  • All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to icbbg2025@gmail.com.
  • Full Refund: Cancellations requested on or before April 4, 2025, will receive a 100% refund, minus a credit card processing fee.
  • Partial Refund or Transfer: Cancellations requested between April 5, 2025, and May 2, 2025, will receive a 50% refund, or you may transfer your registration to another attendee.
  • No Refunds: Cancellations after May 2, 2025, are not eligible for a refund; however, registration transfers to another attendee are still allowed.
